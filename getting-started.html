Getting Started

Here are some simple steps to get started with MCEnrich.com



Step 1

Register a MCEnrich.com account.



Step 2

Install Flash Player.



Step 3

Create your own personal profile (level, subject(s), difficulty level). Start buying digitised assessment titles or question bank to access our huge repository of questions.



Step 4

Attempt questions and view results.



Step 5

Watch out for new available titles from time to time.
