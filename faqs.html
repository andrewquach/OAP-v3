FAQs

Getting  Started

What is the minimum hardware and software configuration required to access MCEnrich.com?

1. Minimum System Requirement:

  • Intel Pentium 4, 3 GHz and above
  • 1 GB Memory
  • 1 Mbps Broadband connection

2. Minimum Software Requirement – Operating System and Browser:

Windows XP SP2 and above:
  • Internet Explorer 7 and above
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome
Mac OS X 10.5 and above:
  • Apple Safari 4 and 5
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome

3. Plugins:

  • Adobe Flash 10.0 and above
  • LEAD Flash Player
Do I need to pay a registration fee?

No, registration is free. You only need to pay for the subscription of assessments, courseware, and question banks.

Creating  Your  Account

How do I start using MCEnrich.com?

1. Register for a MCEnrich.com account: Register here
2. Create your personal profile (E.g. name, email)
3. Subscribe to your desired products (e.g. assessments, courseware, question bank)
4. Create an account for your child with his/her name, date of birth, school, etc.
5. You can now assign the subscribed products to your child.

How many types of accounts are there?

Two. Parent accounts and Child accounts

What is the difference between the two accounts?

Parent Account

  • Create child accounts
  • Subscribe assessments, courseware, and question banks
  • Assign subscribed products to child(ren)
  • Monitor child(ren)’s progress
  • Receive updates

Child Account

  • Assess and complete assessments, courseware, and questions assigned
  • Check their work against the correct answers after submission
  • Not allowed to subscribe to any products
If I have more than one child, how do I register them?

You can create multiple Child accounts by registering each child with a different username and password. Upon registration, each child can use their unique username and password to access their respective accounts.

How many child accounts can one parent account create?

One Parent account can create up to 5 Child accounts.

How many children can one assessment be assigned to?

You can only assign one assessment to one child at a time. Once you re-assign it to another child, the assessment data for the first child will be deleted.

Maintaining  Your  Account

I forgot my username and password, what should I do?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click on “Forgot Username/Password”
3. Enter your username/email
4. An email will be sent to you if you wish to retrieve your username. Alternatively, you may reset your password after entering your username.

How can I track the progress of my child?

Parents can see the test results for the last attempt by clicking on the footprint icon next to the topic. To track your child’s progress, parents can access the “Report” tab to see the topics your child is stronger or weaker in. Remedial practices will be reflected here, and parents can see if the child has improved in his/her weaker areas.

How do I set the areas of focus?

The areas of focus can set in the “Profile” tab; just click on the star beside the topic you want your child to focus on. The “Quiz” tab will generate a quiz based on these focus areas. The child may also “Favorite” challenging questions by clicking on the star next to the questions during the test. These questions will be saved in the “Favorite” tab and can be reviewed later.

How does the “Alert Me” button at every page of my subscribed products work?

With the “Alert Me” button, you are able to track the progress of your child via email. The system will send an email to inform you whenever your child finishes his/her work, as well as his/her scores. It acts like a notification.

Product  Subscription

What methods of payment does MCEnrich.com accept?

You can make payment using a Paypal account (if any) or by Visa, Master, Maestro, or American Express credit and debit cards via Paypal.

Is there a limit to how many assessments, courseware, and question banks I can subscribe to?

No, there is no limit to the number of assessments, courseware, and question banks subscribed to.

Can I get a refund for subscribed products?

No. Once you have subscribed to a product, no refund will be available, regardless of whether the assessments, courseware, or question banks have been done. Please reconfirm your orders before checking out to avoid subscribing to the wrong products.

How safe is my credit/debit card information with MCEnrich.com?

MCEnrich.com will only store your personal particulars in the system, and this personal information will be protected. No payment information, such as credit/debit card numbers and expiry dates, will be stored in the system.

Using  the  Assessments  and  Question  Banks

Can I check what mistakes my child made in the practices, using my account?

Yes, you can view your child's results and practices using your account.

Can my child resume the practice if he/she could not finish on the first attempt?

Yes, as long as your child did not submit his/her answers, he/she will be able to resume the uncompleted practice by saving their work in the question summary menu. Incomplete practices that have been saved will be indicated by a pause symbol.

If my child did not perform well in the practices, can he/she retake the practices again?

Yes, your child may retake the practices after submission by clicking the retake button in the question summary menu.
